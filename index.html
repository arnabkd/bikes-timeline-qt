Welcome to BikeRacks - timeline

Background

My master's thesis involves using machine learning to predict traffic patterns in bikeshare systems across the globe. As part of that work, I have a dataset for the bikeshare system in Oslo (currently operated by ClearChannel). The dataset includes locations, capacities and rack status for all racks in the Oslo area between April and November 2013
